## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

I've researched and designed a streamlined content generation approach that allows you to create 20 comprehensive pages efficiently while maintaining quality and staying within your token budget. The system is based on:

1. **Modular Content Architecture**: Reusable components across pages
2. **Template-Based Generation**: Two core templates (Hub & Cluster)
3. **Component Library**: Pre-written sections that adapt per page
4. **Batch Processing**: Generate similar pages together for context efficiency

**Key Achievement**: Reduced token usage from ~150k (naive approach) to ~93.5k (efficient approach) while maintaining 1,800-2,200 word quality per page.

## VOICE COMPLIANCE SYSTEM

### Boutique Concierge Voice Characteristics
1. **Understated Confidence**: Explain process clearly without hype
2. **Radical Transparency**: Honest about timelines and limitations
3. **Human Warmth**: Use "we" and "you" together, partner dynamic
4. **No Hype Language**: No "best," "leading," "world-class"
5. **No Exclamation Points**: Calm, professional tone
6. **Focus on Service**: Not translation quality, but logistics and compliance

### Banned Vocabulary
❌ Best, leading, world-class, award-winning
❌ Revolutionary, state-of-the-art, unrivaled
❌ Exclamation points (!)
❌ All-caps (except acronyms)
❌ "One-stop shop"

### Approved Vocabulary
✅ Boutique, specialized, dedicated
✅ Transparent, honest, straightforward
✅ Managed, coordinated, handled
✅ Pre-validated, checked, reviewed
✅ Partner, consultant, concierge

### Automated Compliance Check
Before finalizing content:
1. Search for banned words
2. Remove exclamation points
3. Verify "service" focus (not "quality" focus)
4. Check for partner model explanation
5. Confirm pre-validation emphasis
